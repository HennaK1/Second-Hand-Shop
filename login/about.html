About Us


Buy, Sell, Share.
Simple as that! Happy Clothes is the leading app for pre-loved second hand fashion. It is dedicated to transforming the second hand clothes industry for a more sustainable way. We are ecological and want to bring our flea market to the internet available for every one.

You just have to sign up and start shopping. You can find varied categories of clothes and send the seller chat message.
When you want to sell your own pre-used clothes - you are in the right place. Selling on our site is just easy as buying clothes for yourself.
